Help Us Know you More

If you've been job hunting and prepping for interviews, chances are you've started to notice that some of the same questions keep popping up. One of the questions that an interviewer will typically ask at the end of a job interview is whether there is something else that you would like to share, or anything else that the interviewer should know about you.

Most likely, by the time you hear this question, you've already spoken for a while and answered many questions about your skills and experience. It can be tempting to just politely respond by saying that you feel like everything's been covered. Resist this urge. Instead, use this as an opportunity to close the interview on a strong note.
